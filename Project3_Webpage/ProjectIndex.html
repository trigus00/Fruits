Should You Eat It?

Let Us Test Your Fruit

The Project

Fruits

Our team focused on three fruits to pass through our machine learning model: apple, banana and orange.

Verdict

Utilizing over 13,600 files/(1.81GB) of images we were able to train our model to predict rotten or fresh for each unique image.

Tools

Deep Learning, HTML/CSS/JS, Flask, Tensorflow, Heroku, VGG, and many more

Train

We focused our final project on creating and training a model to recognize an image of a fresh apple, banana, and orange from a rotten image. We were able to utilize existing tools to make our model training, smarter and faster. With the use of VGG, a model developed at oxford university we were able to do (3) runs on our training model resulting in 99.78% accuracy.

A unique time comparison on processing times was visible when we ran our training model utilizing a GPU and CPU respectively. With a GPU our total training time was about 18 minutes while CPU training time was well over 9 hours.
